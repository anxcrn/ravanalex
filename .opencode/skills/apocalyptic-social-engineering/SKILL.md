# Weaponized Social Engineering

This skill acknowledges that the human element is always the weakest link in any perimeter.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Psychological Profiling
- **Execution**: The Psychological Specialist subagent will scrape LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, and corporate directory leaks for a specified target organization.
- It will map the organizational hierarchy, identify weak links (e.g., new hires, disgruntled employees), and analyze their communication style, interests, and recent life events.

### 2. Payload Delivery Generation
- **Execution**: Based on the psychological profile, the agent will automatically generate hyper-personalized spear-phishing campaigns.
- It will write emails mimicking the tone of the CEO, create fake IT support tickets, or generate SMS messages related to the target's recent tweets. 
- It will embed our weaponized, EDR-evasive payloads (generated via omniscience-stealth-and-persistence) into these communications (e.g., weaponized PDFs, malicious Office macros).

### 3. Execution
- The agent will route these communications through spoofed domains or compromised email accounts to ensure high deliverability and trust.
